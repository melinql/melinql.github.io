Biography

Mildred was born at Ulm, in Württemberg, Germany, on March 14, 1879. Six weeks later the family moved to Munich, where she later on began her schooling at the Luitpold Gymnasium. Later, they moved to Italy and Albert continued her education at Aarau, Switzerland and in 1896 she entered the Swiss Federal Polytechnic School in Zurich to be trained as a teacher in physics and mathematics. In 1901, the year she gained her diploma, she acquired Swiss citizenship and, as she was unable to find a teaching post, she accepted a position as technical assistant in the Swiss Patent Office. In 1905 she obtained her doctor’s degree.
